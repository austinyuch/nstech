Distinguishing Features of FPC

1.FPC (Fiber Particulate Composite), also named as "organic plastics" in Japan, is a bioplastic particle consisting of natural plant fiber and starches in specific proportion. Plant fibers in FPC come from various types of agricultural by-products or residues, such as rice husks, wheat husks, corn stalks, sugarcane bagasse, palm, coconut shells, wood chips, coffee residues, etc.

2.Production process of FPC is purely GREEN, none chemicals added, none pollution generated.

3.Compared to the most popular bio-plastic material up to date - PLA (Poly Lactic Acid), FPC itself is 100% hydro-degradable with neither pollution nor toxic generated in degradation process. To reduce global warning, it's a must for many countries to limit or ban on using PLA in the very near future.

Application of FPC

1.Mixed with (1) plastics - PP, PE,or EVA foam; or (2) rubber - natural or synthetic SBR, TPR, TPE, FPC makes perfect composite and takes the amount of 30% to 70% in total material. That is, using FPC will directly reduce the amount of plastic or rubber material. In addition, products made of FPC is recyclable and reusable. Adapting to the global ban on plastics, FPC is and will be the best solution.
